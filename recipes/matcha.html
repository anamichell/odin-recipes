Matcha Cookies

Enjoy your afternoon with a plate of matcha cookies. The earthy, grassy tea flavor of matcha is perfectly paired with the sweet and common ingredient, chocolate. These two distinct flavors go hand-in-hand to create a unique flavor that cannot be described. Try something new with this recipe that tastes as good as it looks. Recipe from @bakes_by_brea on Instagram!

Ingredients (6 cookies)

  • 135g unsalted butter
  • 50g brown sugar
  • 40g white sugar
  • 75g egg
  • 3g vanilla extract
  • 240g plain flour
  • 5g cornstarch
  • 3g baking powder
  • 10g matcha
  • 120g white choco chips

Steps

  1. Beat together butter, brown sugar and white sugar.
  2. Mix in the egg in 3 parts, and add vanilla extract
  3. In another bowl, mix flour, cornstarch, baking powder and matcha together.
  4. Add dry ingredients mixture to the wet ingereidents mixture in 3 parts.
  5. Add the white choco chips.
  6. Weigh appox. 115g of cookie dough and shape into a ball.
  7. Refrigerate overnight.
  8. Bake at 190 Celsius for 16 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!

Tips for big and soft cookies from bakes_by_brea

"Make sure the butter isnt too soft, it technically works best with cold butter but when i use cold butter straight from the fridge i find it hard to beat it with a handheld mixer! But if you have a stand mixer defo use cold butter. Make sure to refrigerate/freeze the cookie dough once you balled them up, this helps to keep their height when baked!"
