Red Velvet Cookies with Cream Cheese

Another set of beautiful colored cookies from Brea! Enjoy this classic recipe with cream cheese chocolate chips that enhance the cookies sweetness and elevates your taste buds with every bite with you take. Recipe from @bakes_by_brea on Instagram!

Ingredients (14 cookies)

  • 270g unsalted butter
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 80g white sugar
  • 3 small eggs (approx. 150g)
  • 6ml vanilla extract
  • 20g red food colouring
  • 540g plain flour
  • 10g cornstarch
  • 6g baking powder
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 226g Hershey's cream cheese baking chips

Steps

  1. Beat together butter, brown sugar, and white sugar.
  2. Mix in the egg in 3 parts.
  3. Add vanilla extract and red food colouring to the mixture.
  4. In another bowl, mix flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and cocoa powder together.
  5. Add dry ingredients mixture to the wet ingredients mixture in 3 parts.
  6. Add the cream cheese baking chips and mix.
  7. Weigh appox. 100g of cookie dough and make into a ball shape.
  8. Freeze for 1 hour.
  9. Bake at 190 Celsius for 18 minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
