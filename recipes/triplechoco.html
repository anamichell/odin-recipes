Triple Chocolate Skillet Cookie

Triple Chocolate Skillet Cookie is a quick easy recipe that satisfies your sweet cravings. This recipe combines cocoa powder, chocolate chunks, and chocolate chips to create the pefect cookie. Crispy on the edges and gooey at the center, it's the recipe that have you coming back for more! Recipe from @aymenacooks on TikTok!

Ingredients

  • 125g salted butter
  • 100g light brown sugar
  • 125g caster/granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 175g self raising flour
  • 50g cocoa powder
  • chocolate chunks
  • white chocolate chips
  • ice cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Melt the butter.
  2. Add the melted butter, light brown sugar, and caster/granulated sugar into a seperate bigger bowl.
  3. Mix well until it is all combined.
  4. Add an egg and vanilla extract into the mixture, and mix.
  5. Once everything is combined, add the self raising flour and cocoa powder.
  6. Mix, mix, mix.
  7. Next, add chocolate chunks! Dark chocolate, milk chocolate, the choice is yours.
  8. Add white chocolate chips with it. Then combine.
  9. Add it to a baking tray/skillet.
  10. Add more chocolate chips on top, then bake in oven at 180 Celsius for 20-25 minutes.
  11. Enjoy!
